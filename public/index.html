❋ The Sacred Abode ❋

Guruvayoor Temple

ഗുരുവായൂർ ക്ഷേത്രം

Among the most sacred Vaishnavite temples in India, Guruvayoor Temple stands as an eternal divine flame in the heart of Kerala. Dedicated to Lord Guruvayurappan — a form of Lord Vishnu — this ancient temple holds the power to bless every sacred union performed within its divine presence.

The golden gopuram reaches towards the heavens, while the eternal flame of the temple lamp has burned uninterrupted for centuries. To be married here is to receive the highest divine blessing.
